# Become an AI Engineer

The demand for software engineers who understand how to build with Large Language Models is exploding. To help bridge this skills gap, RantAI Academy is officially opening enrollment for our **Enterprise AI Engineering Bootcamp**.

## Curriculum Overview

This is not a theoretical machine learning course. This is an intensive, practical engineering bootcamp focused entirely on building and deploying production AI systems.

### Module 1: Foundation Models & Prompt Engineering
- The LLM landscape (API providers vs. Open Weights)
- Advanced prompting techniques (Chain of Thought, ReAct)
- Output parsing and structured generation

### Module 2: Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Vector databases and embedding models
- Chunking strategies and metadata filtering
- Evaluate RAG pipelines (RAGAS framework)

### Module 3: Agents & Tool Calling
- Building stateful agents
- Connecting LLMs to external APIs and databases
- Orchestration frameworks (LangChain, LlamaIndex, custom loops)

### Module 4: Production Deployment & MLOps
- Managing prompt versioning
- Telemetry, tracing, and cost monitoring
- Security, prompt injection, and red-teaming

## Who is this for?

This bootcamp is designed for software engineers with at least 1-2 years of programming experience (preferably in Python or TypeScript) who want to transition into the AI engineering space.

Classes start next month. Space is highly limited to ensure a high instructor-to-student ratio.
