# Democratizing AI Creation with ZeroCode

For the past two years, RantAI has been focused on providing enterprise-grade AI infrastructure for engineering teams. Today, we are expanding our mission to empower *everyone* in the organization to build with AI.

Say hello to **ZeroCode**.

## Visual AI Development

ZeroCode is a visual, drag-and-drop platform for building complex AI workflows, chatbots, and generative data pipelines without writing a single line of code.

### Everything You Need, In One Canvas

- **Pre-built Templates**: Start quickly with curated templates for customer support, HR onboarding, content generation, and more.
- **Knowledge Bases**: Simply upload your PDFs, Word documents, or connect to Notion and Google Drive. ZeroCode handles the vectorization and chunking automatically in the background.
- **Custom Logic**: Use our visual flow builder to define precise conditional logic and multi-step processes for your AI applications.

## Enterprise Security Included

Just because it's no-code doesn't mean it sacrifices compliance. Every app built on ZeroCode inherits RantAI's enterprise security posture:
- End-to-end encryption
- No training on customer data
- Granular Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
- Full audit logs

ZeroCode is available today for all active RantAI Enterprise customers. Log into your dashboard to start building.
